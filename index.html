Guildmaster

Welcome to the city! You're a newly arrived merchant with only a few silver coins. The city was recently liberated from the barbarian hordes and the economy needs to be revitalized. This is where you come in.

First, make some gold selling common goods. Then, choose what to build next, what to sell, who to hire and what your target market is for each week.

As unique situations present themselves, manage your assets wisely. Not all opportunities are as lucrative as they may seem. Also remember that while basic needs are always in demand, you'll need to build a reputation before trying to sell higher quality items.

Will you play it safe or enter the market aggressively? Meet the needs of the common folk or find a niche market in more affluent circles?

The choice is yours!
